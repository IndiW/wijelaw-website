Our Firm

At Wijesundera & Associates, we are dedicated to superior results and exemplary client service. Our commitment is to offer comprehensive solutions at reasonable rates. We know our clients do not appreciate wasted time, vague answers nor elaborate schemes. We strive to provide clients with straightforward answers and practical solutions to even the most complex problems. At Wijesundera & Associates, we have developed specialized expertise in a number of key practices. We will connect you with a lawyer who is fully prepared to resolve your legal issue, and provide you with the attentive, results-oriented representation you require at a reasonable cost. Our initial consultations are offered free or at a reduced rate.

Neil had his primary education at the S. Thomas’ College in Mt.Lavinia, in Sri Lanka. In 1986 upon completion of his law studies at the Sri Lanka Law College he was called to the bar as an Attorney-At-Law of the Supreme Court of Sri Lanka. He then continued his law studies in England and in 1990 was awarded the Master of Laws by the Faculty of Laws of the University of London, England. In 1997 he was called to the Bar as a Solicitor of the Supreme Court of England & Wales.Upon successful completion of the prescribed qualifying examinations and the Bar Admission Course of the Law Society of Upper Canada he was admitted as Barrister-At-Law and Solicitor of the Superior Court of Justice in Ontario, in 2002. He has practiced in the areas of Criminal Defence, Family and Civil Litigation and at the present time confines himself predominantly to the practice of Real Estate, Corporate Commercial, Wills and Estates.

Janaki is an Attorney-at-Law of the Supreme Court of Sri Lanka and a Solicitor of England & Wales. She received her Master of Law degree from the University of London, U.K. In Canada, she completed her Articles of Clerkship with Diamond & Diamond, Barristers, and was called to the Bar in 2003. Prior to her call, she has worked in the areas of real estate, civil litigation, immigration and personal injury in large and specialist boutique law firms in London (England) and Toronto.
Janaki has practiced family law since 2003. She has extensive experience in negotiating separation and co-habitation agreements, marriage contracts and resolving complex support, property, custody and access disputes, and obtaining Divorce Orders. She represents clients in Superior Court of Justice, Ontario Court, Family Court, and at mediations and arbitrations. She has practiced Immigration Law for over 20 years. She is proficient in obtaining permanent (family sponsorship/skilled worker/business/investor) and temporary (study permit/work permit/visitor visa) residence in Canada. She also helps clients with complex Citizenship issues, Permanent Resident Card extensions and immigration appeals.
